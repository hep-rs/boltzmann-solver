pub fn t_range(s: f64, m1: f64, m2: f64, m3: f64, m4: f64) -> (f64, f64)

Return the minimum and maximum value of the Mandelstam variable \(t\) based on the four particle squared masses \(m_1^2\), \(m_2^2\), \(m_3^2\) and \(m_4^2\), where particles 1 and 2 are initial state and particles 3 and 4 are the final state particles.

Explicitly, the values are:

\begin{equation}\begin{aligned} t_{\text{min}} &= \frac{(m_1^2 - m_2^2 - m_3^3 + m_4^4)^2}{4 s} - \frac{\lambda^{\frac{1}{2}}(s, m_1^2, m_2^2) \lambda^{\frac{1}{2}}(s, m_3^2, m_4^2)}{s} \\ t_{\text{max}} &= \frac{(m_1^2 - m_2^2 - m_3^3 + m_4^4)^2}{4 s} \end{aligned}\end{equation}
